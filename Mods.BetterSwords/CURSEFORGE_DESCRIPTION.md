# Better Swords

Enhance your sword combat with stunning visual effects!

## Features

**Sword Trail Effects**
Every sword swing now displays a beautiful charged trail that follows your blade!

**Enhanced Impact Particles**
- Charged sword impact effects
- Blade slash particles
- More satisfying hit feedback

**Omnislash Signature**
Replaces VortexStrike with an anime-style multi-hit combo:
- 4 powerful strikes
- 18 Physical damage per hit
- Lock-on to target

**Improved Thrust**
- 0.3s charge (was 0.7s)
- Stronger dash force
- Quick recovery

## Installation

1. Download and extract to `%APPDATA%\Hytale\UserData\Mods\`
2. Folder should be named `Nakai.BetterSwords`
3. Launch Hytale!

## Compatibility

Hytale Early Access - Works with mods that dont modify sword interactions.
